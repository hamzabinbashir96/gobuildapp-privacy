Delete Your ToneFix Account

To request deletion of your ToneFix account and associated data, send an email to:

support.gobuildapp@gmail.com

Subject: ToneFix Account Deletion Request

Include your registered email address in the email.

What gets deleted:

  • Your Firebase authentication account
  • Your usage data stored in Firestore
  • Your VIP status and subscription data
  • Your ad reward tracking data

Retention period:

All data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your request.

Data kept:

Anonymous aggregated usage statistics may be retained for analytics purposes.
